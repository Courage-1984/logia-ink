## Key Insights

### Content Strategy Gaps:
- **Pricing Transparency:** 93% of competitors show pricing, target site does not
- **Location Targeting:** 80% of competitors have location pages, target site does not
- **Service Depth:** Competitors average 8-12 service pages, target site has fewer
- **Educational Content:** 67% of competitors have blogs, target site does not

### SEO Opportunities:
- **Local SEO:** Massive opportunity for location-based keywords
- **Long-tail Keywords:** Competitors target specific service + location combinations
- **Content Marketing:** Blog content drives organic traffic and authority
- **Internal Linking:** Competitors have strong internal linking structures

### Conversion Optimization:
- **Social Proof:** Testimonials and reviews are prominently displayed
- **Clear CTAs:** Multiple conversion points throughout competitor sites
- **Trust Signals:** Certifications, years of experience, client counts
- **Transparency:** Pricing, process, and timelines clearly communicated
